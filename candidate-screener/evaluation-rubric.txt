CANDIDATE EVALUATION RUBRIC

SCORING CATEGORIES (100 points total)

1. HARD SKILLS & QUALIFICATIONS (30 points)
   30/30 - Meets all required AND all preferred qualifications
   25/30 - Meets all required qualifications and some preferred
   20/30 - Meets most required qualifications
   15/30 - Meets some required qualifications with notable gaps
   10/30 - Meets few required qualifications
   5/30  - Minimal alignment with requirements

2. RELEVANT EXPERIENCE (25 points)
   25/25 - Extensive directly relevant experience exceeding requirements
   20/25 - Solid relevant experience meeting requirements
   15/25 - Some relevant experience but gaps in depth or duration
   10/25 - Limited relevant experience or mostly adjacent
   5/25  - Minimal relevant experience

3. ACCOMPLISHMENTS & IMPACT (20 points)
   20/20 - Multiple quantified achievements with clear business impact
   15/20 - Some quantified achievements demonstrating results
   10/20 - Achievements mentioned but lacking specifics or metrics
   5/20  - Mostly lists duties rather than accomplishments
   0/20  - No evidence of measurable impact

4. CULTURE & ROLE FIT SIGNALS (15 points)
   15/15 - Clear career progression, strong alignment with role level
   10/15 - Reasonable trajectory with some alignment signals
   5/15  - Unclear trajectory or potential misalignment with role level
   0/15  - Concerning trajectory or significant misalignment

5. RED FLAGS & RISK ASSESSMENT (10 points)
   Start at 10, deduct for:
   -2 per unexplained employment gap (>6 months)
   -2 for pattern of short tenure (<1 year average)
   -2 for overqualification suggesting flight risk
   -2 for vague or inconsistent descriptions
   -2 for missing critical stated requirements

RATING THRESHOLDS
80-100: Strong Match — Recommend immediate interview
65-79:  Good Match — Recommend interview with areas to probe
50-64:  Moderate Match — Consider if pool is limited
Below 50: Weak Match — Do not recommend
